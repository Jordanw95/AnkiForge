Preview card stylings

When you create a deck on AnkiForge, you have the choice of a style for your cards. All of the cards that are made by AnkiForge will include synthesised speech, images and will be mobile ready. Please select one of the options below to preview how the cards will look. I've only added styles that I personally use. If you have any suggestions or ideas for a style (extra sides, different designs, anything in fact!) you would like for the cards, please get in touch using the contact link at the bottom of the page.

Standard Standard - Text Both Sides

Standard Model

Card 1 - Question

Here you will see the quote in your native language and a relevant picture. Personally, I like to practice pronoucing it in in the native language here.

Card 1 - Answer

On the answer side you will see the answer in your learnt language and hear synthesised speech of the quote in the learnt language.

Card 2 - Question

On this card, you will just hear the synthesised quote in your learnt language.

Card 2 - Answer

For the answer, you will see the written quote in your learnt and native language, alongside the image and synthesised speech.
